About Collegiate

Built on Honesty.
Powered by Hustle.

Hi, I'm Loren. I'm a college student and a Spokane local who started Collegiate Home Services to prove something: that showing up on time, communicating clearly, and doing the work right the first time is rare enough to build a business around.

With 5 years of hands-on trade experience and a lifetime of solving problems, I bring more than just tools to the job. I bring the kind of attention and care that most homeowners have stopped expecting.

Pricing is simple. Book a half day or a full day. You get my complete focus, quality workmanship, and I will not leave until the job is done right.

The name says it all. Collegiate.

This business is tied to college life because I believe the trades offer something that a white collar path cannot always promise: the direct, honest reward of doing something with your hands and doing it well. Collegiate Home Services is proof of that.
